What's new?

Latest comments module: choose whether to show excerpts or the full text of the comment. You can decide whether to show the entire comment or just an exceprt. Excerpts can be configured with a maximum number of words and a maximum number of characters (whichever comes first).

Latest comments module: article title display options. You can now choose whether to display the article's title, and whether it should be a link if shown.

Article comment options: choose whether to auto-publish comments or not, overriding global setting. Up until now this was only a global option, available in the component's Options page. You can now choose to have specific articles auto-publish comments or have them unpublished by default until a comments moderator approves them.

Bug fixes and minor improvements. Please take a look at the CHANGELOG below.
